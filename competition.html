Competition Format

The HUMS International Science Bowl is designed to challenge and inspire students through a unique two-stage format:

  • Team Formation: Each team can have up to 2-4 individuals.
  • Individual Preliminary Round: All team members compete individually in a preliminary round.
  • Team Score Calculation: Each member's individual score is added up to form the team's total score.
  • Advancement: The top 16 teams with the highest combined scores advance to the Bowl round.
  • Bowl Round: In the Bowl, teams compete head-to-head against other top teams in a bracket-style tourney. Teams that lose a match will have a chance to play in the "Loser Bracket."

Disclaimer: There willl be extensive measures in place to prevent cheating. If you are caught cheating, you will be banned from participating in any future tourneys and will have your team's score be nullified.
