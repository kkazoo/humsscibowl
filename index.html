Welcome to the HUMS International Science Bowl
The HUMS International Science Bowl is a global competition designed to ignite curiosity and inspire excellence in science. Open to high school students worldwide, the event features challenging individual and team rounds in topics like biology, genetics, anatomy, physiology and more. Compete with top students, win scholarships, and connect with a vibrant community passionate about science and discovery.
