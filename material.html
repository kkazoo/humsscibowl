Topics Covered

The preliminary round and the Bowl will cover a broad range of science topics, including:

  • Anatomy & Physiology

  • Cell Biology
  • Ecology
  • Genetics
  • Microbiology
  • Plants

Recommended Textbooks

  • Campbell Biology (Urry et al.)
  • Essential Cell Biology (Alberts et al.)

These textbooks are not required, but are highly recommended for thorough preparation.
